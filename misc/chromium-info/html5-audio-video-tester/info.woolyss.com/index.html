About

Site

The "woolyss" word means nothing at all. Because it was unique on the web in 2007, Jerry (admin) chose this domain name. This website exists since 2007. It was hosted on another web service provider (Free ISP) until 2010.

Administrator

Hello! ;)
My name is Jerry. As a non-professional developer, I write code in my spare time. In general, I develop all "handly" (without using of CMS and framework). I speak English and French. Please, follow the stars in the sky to contact me! ^^

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is very important to me, I treat it like I would like mine to be treated. This privacy policy document explains when and how personal information may be collected. In any case, only non-personally identifiable information is collected, and I never sell, share, trade, or otherwise transfer personal information to third parties.

In few words, this website and its sub-domains use:
  • no advertisement and sponsored content
  • no web analytics
  • no pop-up window
  • no tracking tool
  • no bundled software
  • no malicious redirection and shortened URL
  • no third-party resource
  • no registration required
  • no HTML frame
  • no CAPTCHA
  • no Push notification

Server logs

In accordance with the European Union (EU) law, only server logs are created and stored (like each Web server in the world does) during maximum 1 year by my Web service provider • [FR] LCEN).

GDPR

This website is GDPR and CCPA compliant.

Cookies

Only session cookies are used by the comment system to store temporary non-personally identifiable information directly in your browser when you write a comment on the website.

Comment system

  • Your personal user data are neither stored nor shared. Obviously, your username and comment are saved forever. No user profile is created.
  • Your email address will be removed after 1 week if no exchange. Otherwise, it is only kept in my internal messaging service.
  • Your comment will be automatically approved but not editable.
  • Your avatar will be automatically created by an internal process (no third-party service).

Browser extensions

My browser extensions do not collect personal information, do not collect your browsing history, and do not track your browsing activity. They do not collect data of any sort, and they do not inject ads.

Changes

I may update this privacy policy from time to time in order to reflect, for example, changes to my practices or for other operational, legal or regulatory reasons.
