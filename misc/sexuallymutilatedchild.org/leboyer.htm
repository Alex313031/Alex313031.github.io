Dr. Frederick Leboyer is the founder of the Leboyer philosophy of childbirth and author of Birth Without Violence (Knopf, 1975) and Loving Hands (Knopf, 1976). Rosemary Romberg Weiner is the author of Circumcision: The Painful Dilemma (Bergin & Garvey, 1985). Dr. Leboyer's letter to her was typed on both sides of the page. It is reproduced here in its entirety, rearranged to fit a computer screen.
