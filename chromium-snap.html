A quick note about Chromium

Chromium is brought to you by the Ubuntu Desktop team at Canonical.

Chromium is now delivered as a snap package. This enables users to receive security fixes, updates and new features, faster. It also allows Canonical to maintain a single Chromium package, reducing maintenance overhead. The snap is installable across all supported releases of Ubuntu, derivatives, and many other Linux distributions.

Your bookmarks, history, and other data have been transferred automatically.

We’re aware of some issues that we’re working hard to resolve. For further details, check out what we’ve done and how you can give feedback.
