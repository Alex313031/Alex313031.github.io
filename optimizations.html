Thorium Compiler Optimizations

Document explaining the compiler optimizations of Thorium; what they do and how they work.

  Thorium uses many modifications to the compiler BUILD.gn file, which highly optimize the binaries, at the cost of size. While Google tries to minimize size, Thorium takes up approximately ~250MB compared to ~150MB for Chrome (Note that the full Thorium installer is ~500MB because it includes other stuff like thorium_shell and chromedriver). Neither of these is very large, and me and many others appreciate speed and performance over the smallest size.

  A highlight of these optimizations, and the first we will discuss, is AVX.
