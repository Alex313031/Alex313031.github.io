Thorium Performance

Performance comparisons using two popular benchmarks, Speedometer and Octane. The leftside images are Chromium, and the rightside images are Thorium. Testing was done on my main machine: AMD FX-8370 overclocked to 4.5 Ghz. | 24 GB 1600 Mhz. DDR3 RAM | Nvidia GTX 970 | Gigabyte GA-78LMT-USB3 R2 Motherboard. The browsers were run on a fresh, updated install of Ubuntu 22.04.1, codename "Jammy Jellyfish" with no other major background tasks runnning.
