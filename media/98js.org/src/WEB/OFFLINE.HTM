Thank you for your interest in Support Online.

To access Support Online, you need to be connected to the Internet. Windows 98 has detected that you currently do not have an Internet connection set up.

Connect me to the Internet.
If you have never connected to the Internet before, the Internet Connection wizard will help you sign up for Internet access. After you are connected to the Internet, your Internet browser will bring up the Support Online site. If you believe that you have already established an Internet connection, click this link to retry to connect to Support Online.

I will connect to Support Online later.
If you do not want to connect to Support Online at this time, click this link to close the browser window. When you are ready to return to Support Online, you can choose to create a new connection to the Internet at any time by doing the following:

  • Click Start.
  • Point to Programs, point to Internet Explorer, and then click on Connection Wizard.
  • The Internet Connection wizard will help you select an Internet Service Provider (ISP) or connect you to your Local Area Network (LAN).
